Simple date parser

GitHub: https://Artik-Man.github.io/simple-date-parser/
NPM: https://www.npmjs.com/package/simple-date-parser

Supported date formats

  • dd MMM YYYY hh:mm:ss a
  • MM dd YYYY hh:mm:ss a
  • dd MM YYYY hh:mm:ss
  • YYYY MM dd hh:mm:ss a
  • YYYYMMdd
  • [today|yesterday|...] hh:mm:ss a
  • ...

If your date format is not support now, please tell me here
